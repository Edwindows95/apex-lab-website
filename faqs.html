How It Works

Apex Lab connects your Garmin wearable data with your work calendar to show how your body responds to your workday. We analyze heart rate, stress, Body Battery, sleep, HRV, and respiration — all linked to your actual meetings, tasks, and routines — and deliver personalized insights.

Garmin & Technology

Most modern Garmin watches that support Labfront are compatible, including models from the Venu, Forerunner, and Fenix series. We can advise on specific compatibility before you start.

Labfront is a research-grade data collection platform. It syncs with your Garmin to securely capture health data in the background. It's used by researchers worldwide for wearable studies.

You'll need the Garmin Connect app (which you likely already have) and the Labfront app. Setup takes about 15 minutes with our guided instructions.

The impact on battery life is minimal. Most Garmin devices maintain their normal battery performance while collecting data through Labfront.

For Teams

Each team member gets their own Garmin and individual setup. After 2–4 weeks of tracking, individual participants receive their own private reports. The organization receives only an anonymized, aggregate analysis of team patterns.

Aggregate patterns only — like average team stress levels during meeting-heavy days, or group-level recovery metrics. Never individual names, data, or identifiable information.

We recommend 5–10 participants for a meaningful pilot. Larger teams can participate in phased rollouts. Contact us to discuss what works for your organization.

Absolutely. Every participant gives their own informed consent. No one is required to participate, and anyone can withdraw at any time without consequences.

Your report covers stress patterns, recovery quality, sleep analysis, Body Battery trends, work-activity correlations, and personalized recommendations. It includes clear visualizations and plain-language interpretations.

Reports are typically delivered within 1–2 weeks after the measurement period ends, depending on data quality and scope of analysis.
