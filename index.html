How it works

We collect physiological data from Garmin wearables through Labfront — including heart rate, stress levels, Body Battery, respiration, sleep, and optionally HRV. We also integrate your work calendar (ICS format) to map activities to health responses.

Most modern Garmin watches that support Labfront are compatible. This includes popular models from the Venu, Forerunner, and Fenix series. We can advise on specific compatibility before starting.

A typical measurement period runs for 2 to 4 weeks. This gives us enough data to identify meaningful patterns in your stress, recovery, and work-related health responses. Longer tracking is available for longitudinal insights.

Privacy

No. Individual reports belong to the participant. If an organization commissions the study, they only receive anonymized, aggregate-level insights — never personal data. Privacy is fundamental to how Apex Lab operates.

Yes. All data handling follows GDPR requirements. We only collect data that is necessary for the analysis, participants give explicit informed consent, and data is stored securely with full transparency on processing.
