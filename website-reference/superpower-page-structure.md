# Superpower.com — Page Structure Analysis
Reference for Apex Lab website rebuild

## Homepage Section Flow (top to bottom)

1. **Hero** — Bold headline + 3 value props + single CTA + trust badge (HSA/FSA)
2. **Quick wins strip** — 3 icons: No wait times / Fast results / Simple & convenient
3. **Product showcase** — "It starts with 100+ labs" + scrolling product UI screenshots
4. **Social proof quote** — Single powerful testimonial in large type
5. **Stats bar** — 3 animated counters (62% diabetes, 48% heart disease, 71% aging)
6. **How it works** — 3 numbered steps with images
7. **Video testimonials** — Horizontal scroll of influencer/athlete video cards
8. **Use cases** — "Just test to make it clear" — 3 persona cards with biomarker + recommendation
9. **What's included** — 6 feature cards in grid (data, uploads, plan, messaging, add-ons, clinic)
10. **Authority** — "Developed by world-class medical professionals" + university logos + 4 doctor profiles
11. **Pricing** — Membership card with product images + pricing breakdown + payment options
12. **FAQ** — Expandable accordion grouped by topic
13. **Biomarker browser** — Filterable list of 100+ biomarkers by health category
14. **Final CTA** — Full-width hero image + emotional headline + CTA
15. **Footer** — Mega footer with product/company/compare/library/partnerships/connect columns

## Key Design Patterns to Adapt for Apex Lab

### What works well:
- **Single font family** (Nb International Pro) for both headings and body — clean, unified
- **Pill-shaped CTAs** (border-radius: 9999px) — modern, friendly
- **Stats with animated counters** — instant credibility
- **How it works in 3 steps** — simple, scannable
- **Product UI screenshots** as proof the product exists and works
- **Authority section** with logos + doctor headshots
- **FAQ for SEO** — massive keyword-rich content

### Apex Lab adaptations:
- Replace orange (#FC5F2B) energy with Stone Noir restraint (#4A4640)
- Replace "100+ biomarkers" messaging with "continuous 24/7 wearable + calendar data"
- Replace blood test personas with work stress personas (executive, team lead, remote worker)
- Replace doctor authority with sport science / PT credentials
- Replace pricing card with engagement model (consultation → screening → 12-week plan → evaluation)
- Keep: stats bar, how-it-works flow, product screenshots, FAQ, pill CTAs
