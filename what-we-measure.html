HRV

Heart Rate Variability is the gold standard for measuring recovery. Higher overnight HRV indicates better readiness and resilience to work stress.

The lineage
of your data.

01

Collection

Raw PPG and accelerometry signals are captured passively by your Garmin wearable, worn 24/7 throughout the measurement period.

02

Refinement

Signals are exported through Labfront for research-grade artifact removal and baseline normalization before analysis.

03

Intelligence

Apex Lab layers your ICS calendar on top of the physiological data, generating actionable insights on how work affects your body.
